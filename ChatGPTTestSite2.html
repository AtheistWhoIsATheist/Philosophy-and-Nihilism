The Religious Experience of Nihilism

“...turn us into melancholy metaphysicians. The pride of life and glory of the world will shrivel. It is after all but the standing quarrel of hot youth and hoary old. Old age has the last word: the purely naturalistic look at life however enthusiastically it may begin is sure to end in sadness. This sadness lies at the heart of every merely positivistic, agnostic, or naturalistic scheme of philosophy...” - James

"To this extent, nihilism, as the denial of a truthful world of being, might be a divine way of thinking." - Nietzsche

This is a work of infinite ambition and utter worthlessness; a work of questions rather than answers. This is an interpretation of Nihilism that finds the ‘everyday’ view, the mainstream philosophical definitions, misguided at best...
